About Us

Established in 2015, H&N Engineering is a leading service provider in the Mechanical and Electrical (M&E) service, installation and maintenance sector. Our team consists of competent engineers led by project managers with over 20 years of experience. Our expertise spans across Air Conditioning & Mechanical Ventilation Systems (ACMV), Electrical Systems, Fire Alarm & Fire Protection Systems and Building Management Systems.

 Mission

To enhance lives by improving the efficiency of our equipment installation, service, and maintenance. We will remain persistent and consistent, invest in continuous development, understand customer requirements and develop efficient and timely solutions. Our goal is to expand our service network and follow a sustainable approach to achieve our objectives.

Vision

To become the leading, most comprehensive, and flexible Mechanical & Electrical solution provider in and around Singapore, and to be recognized as a renowned industry leader by being the first choice for our customers.

Safety is our no. 1 priority

At H&N Engineering, we believe that safety is our number one priority. Our commitment to maintaining a secure and hazard-free environment is unwavering. Always stay alert, be aware, and ensure not just your safety but also the safety of those around you.
